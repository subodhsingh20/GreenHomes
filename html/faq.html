Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plants do you sell? ▶
We offer a wide variety of indoor, outdoor, flowering, and succulent plants. Each category is curated for different environments and aesthetic needs.
How long does delivery take? ▶
Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days. Remote areas may take up to 7 days.
Can I return a plant if it's damaged? ▶
Yes! If a plant arrives damaged, you can return or exchange it within 3 days. Just send us a photo and your order ID.
Do you offer care tips for plants? ▶
Yes, every plant comes with a care guide. We also have a full "Care Tips" page with detailed watering, light, and soil requirements.
What payment methods do you accept? ▶
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for select locations.
